About OJM UAE Chapter

The UAE chapter of OJM represents the dynamic voice of Mozambican youth in the Emirates, fostering leadership development, cultural preservation, and community engagement through innovative programs and initiatives.

Youth Leadership

Developing the next generation of Mozambican leaders through mentorship and training programs

Cultural Heritage

Preserving and celebrating Mozambican traditions, language, and values in the UAE diaspora

Community Building

Creating strong networks and support systems for Mozambican youth across all emirates

Innovation

Embracing modern approaches to youth engagement while honoring traditional values
